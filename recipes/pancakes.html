Pancakes

Home

Description

Pancakes are made with a mixed batter using flour, milk, eggs, butter, salt, baking powder, and sugar.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • .25 teapons salt, or more for taste
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1.25 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. In large bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. make a well in the center and pour in milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lighly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
